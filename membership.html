G2 Members and Membership

Commitment

To become a member of G2, commitment is your number one asset; Not your voice. G2 believes if you can talk, you can sing: Not just the Talent

Determination

G2 requires members who are not only committed but who are determined to go all the way amidst all the odds; members who will be ready to go the extra-mile

Passionate and Persistent

Members who are passionate about being in G2 and wanting to grow always. Choristers who are persistent amidst all odds.
